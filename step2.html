The art of creating open space.

Step 2 was much different. Joey was skating very well but when he had the puck he slowed to a near stand still. In step 2 Joey learned how to skate with a hockey puck; how to appoach a defensive player and manipulate him to create space. Joey learned to keep his feet moving and his head up when he got the puck. Joey also learned how to steal the puck from other player and got so good at it; the goalies could no longer stop him. After the last practice, Joey's coached talked to his mom and the following week, Joey started step 3.
